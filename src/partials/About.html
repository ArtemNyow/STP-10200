About the game

A Magical Puzzle Adventure
Magic Funfair isn’t just about matching — it’s about discovery. By day, you restore the abandoned fairground, fixing broken rides and unraveling lost stories. At night, new secrets emerge as magical versions of the objects appear for advanced merging and hidden quests.

  • Immersive 3D puzzles with merge mechanics

  • Day-Night switch that changes the game world

  • Fairground setting filled with magical stories and surprises
